## Formula

You can write **inline math** using `\\( a^2 + b^2 = c^2 \\)` which renders as \( a^2 + b^2 = c^2 \).

You can also write **block math** like this:

$$
E = mc^2
$$

Or $\nabla\cdot E$:

$$
\nabla \cdot \mathbf{E} = \frac{\rho}{\varepsilon_0}
$$
